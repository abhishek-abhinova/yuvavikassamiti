About Yuva Vikas Samiti

Empowering communities through sustainable development since 2007

Our Story

Yuva Vikas Samiti is a non-profit organization established in 2007 with a vision to create sustainable change in marginalized communities. We work across 15 districts in Uttar Pradesh, focusing on holistic development through integrated approaches.

Our organization believes in empowering communities from within, building local capacity, and creating lasting impact through collaborative efforts. We address critical issues including health, education, livelihood, women empowerment, and environmental conservation.

Mission

To empower marginalized communities through sustainable development programs focusing on health, education, livelihood, and social justice while promoting gender equality and environmental conservation.
