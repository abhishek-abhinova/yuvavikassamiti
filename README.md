# Yuva Vikas Samiti - Official Website

## About
Yuva Vikas Samiti (YVS) is a non-governmental organization registered in 2007, dedicated to empowering marginalized communities through sustainable development programs. We focus on health, education, livelihood, and social justice while promoting gender equality and environmental conservation.

## Our Mission
To empower marginalized communities through sustainable development programs focusing on health, education, livelihood, and social justice while promoting gender equality and environmental conservation.

## Our Vision
A society where every individual, regardless of gender, caste, or economic status, has equal opportunities for growth, dignity, and sustainable livelihood in a healthy environment.

## Key Focus Areas
- Health & Nutrition
- Education & Skill Development
- Women Empowerment
- Water & Sanitation (Jal Jeevan Mission)
- Disaster Management
- Agriculture & Livelihood
- Digital Empowerment
- Micro Finance
- HIV/AIDS & TB Prevention
- Rehabilitation Services

## Major Projects
- **Jal Jeevan Mission (ISA)** - Implementation Support Agency for water supply in Ambedkar Nagar & Balrampur districts
- **Bal Gurukul Project** - Quality education program across 7 districts in UP and Jharkhand
- **S3 - Sarva Sakhi Swabhiman** - Women empowerment through skill development
- **AkshayaDhaaga Skill Centre** - Tailoring and skill training for women
- **National Handicrafts Development Programme (NHDP)**
- **Environmental Awareness Programs**
